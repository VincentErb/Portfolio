Generalities

Introduction

This portfolio gathers all the knowledge and skills acquired over the course of my fifth year at INSA Toulouse. From October 2019 to January 2020,
I followed the PTP Innovative Smart Systems (abbreviated ISS). After four years of a classical formation, pursuing a degree in Networking and Computer Science (IR), this last semester constituted a real change. Indeed, the cursus relied on bringing together the skills from people with a background in electronics, computer science, physics, and even business and management. It was a huge opportunity for me to learn new technical and soft skills, and I tried to gather them on this website.

I encourage you to read this portfolio by thematic content. You can navigate from course to course (UF) using the SELECT COURSE button. In each course, you will find a descriptive part, a technical part, and an analytic part, relative to all the classes in the course. You can also navigate in a classical way, choosing to consult the specific part of one course, by using the navigation menus on the right part of the navigation bar.
